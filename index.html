Tunnel Boring Club & Competition

I am part of a Tunnel Boring Club run by CU Boulder Students. Every year we design, build, and compete with a ~12ft long, ~2ft diameter tunnel boring machine. We compete in The Boring Company's "Not-A-Boring-Competition".

Tunnel Support Lead (August 2025 - Present)

  • Leading the tunnel support sub team through the design, testing, and fabrication of a new HVAC duct-based tunnel support system.
  • Utilizing a passive release mechanism with spring plungers so the system relies on the force of the machine moving to pull the tunnel out of the storage section rather than active control.
  • Tested tunnel lining to confirm a Factor of Safety (FOS) for regular soil pressures when mining.
  • Tested abrasion, spring plunger release, and mounting methods.

Mechanical Team Member - Tunnel Support (Sep 2024 - August 2025)

  • Contributed heavily to the design of a brand-new cylindrical axis 3D printer for our tunnel support system.
  • Worked in SolidWorks to design the subsystem, including z, r, and segment ejection systems.
  • Welded, cut, and ground steel parts for assembly.
